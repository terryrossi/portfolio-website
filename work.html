Roll the Dice

This is a Game for 2 players based on random dice rolls. Created in Javascript, HTML5/CSS3.

Player wins when he/she reaches 20.

Player can hit the Roll Button as many times as he/she wants but loses all the points when rolling a 1.

Player must hit the Hold Button to accumulate the score before rolling a 1.
